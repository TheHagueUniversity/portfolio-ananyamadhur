About the project:
As a part of our Bespoke Design assignment, I paired up with a classmate to do a project for Hallmark. The project was to design a bespoke gift giving experience.The project involved designing a card personalisation tool and digital unwrapping.

The main focus of this assignment was to help the giver bring out the emotional message with the card personalisation tool. As a team, we split our work - so I designed the card personalisation tool and my partner designed the digital unwrapping.

The Design Process:
There are 5 phases in the design process. The phases are; Empathise: Define: Ideate: Prototype and Test.

In 2018, I was one amongst 30 young adults selected in my city, Mumbai, for the YLAC (Young Leaders for Active Citizenship) Counter Speech Fellowship.
The fellowship aims to involve creative young people in audio and visual storytelling using social media to create awareness about social issues relevant to them.
Our group worked on 6 themes: Diversity, Sustainability,Gender Equality, Body Positivity, Mental Welbeing and Bullying.Of these, I focused on Diversity, Sustainability,
Gender Equality and Bullying. YLAC partnered with Instagram for this project.
