About the Project:
As a part of our Bespoke Design assignment, I paired up with a classmate to do a project for Hallmark. The project was to design a bespoke gift giving experience. The project involved designing a card personalisation tool and digital unwrapping. The main focus of this assignment was to help the giver bring out the emotional message with the card personalisation tool. As a team, we split our work - so I designed the card personalisation tool and my partner designed the digital unwrapping.

The Design Process:
There are 5 phases in the design process. The phases are; Empathise: Define: Ideate: Prototype and Test.
